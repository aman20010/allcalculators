Atherogenic Index (AIP)
TG/HDL Ratio
AIP = log₁₀(TG / HDL) — both converted to mmol/L

Risk categories:
< 0.11 — Low cardiovascular risk
0.11 – 0.21 — Intermediate risk
> 0.21 — High cardiovascular risk

AIP is a strong predictor of atherosclerosis and coronary artery disease. A lower AIP is better. Consult your cardiologist for clinical interpretation.
